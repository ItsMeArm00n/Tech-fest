Model Architecture

AQIntel is powered by a dual-model system optimized for real-world air quality prediction:

  • Classification Model: A Random Forest Classifier that predicts the overall pollution category (Low, Moderate, High) based on key environmental features like traffic levels, industrial activity, and weather conditions.
  • Regression Model: A Gradient Boosting Regressor trained to predict the exact Air Quality Index (AQI) value using pollutant concentrations (PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, O₃) combined with meteorological data.
🧠 AQIntel combines flexibility, accuracy, and scalability to deliver reliable air pollution insights.
